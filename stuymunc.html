StuyMUNC

StuyMUNC is Stuyvesant High School's spring conference that typically occurs toward the end of April. Designed for both novices and experienced delegates, StuyMUNC offers a variety of General Assembly, Specialized, Crisis, and JCC committees with thought-provoking, divisive topics.
