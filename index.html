Our Conferences

StuyMUN hosts two conferences each year: miniMUNC, a fall novice conference, and StuyMUNC, a spring conference featuring a range of General Assembly, Specialized, and Crisis committees.

Fall · Novice Conference

miniMUNC

miniMUNC introduces new delegates to Model UN through workshops, accessible topics, and supportive chairs, preparing them for the rest of the circuit.

View miniMUNC
Spring · Flagship Conference

StuyMUNC

StuyMUNC is our larger spring conference, designed for both novice and advanced delegates with a wide variety of engaging committees.
