Stuyvesant Model United Nations

StuyMUN is Stuyvesant High School's Model United Nations team. We are student-run and provide a welcoming community for delegates interested in public speaking, diplomacy, and global affairs.

Who We Are

We attend both local and collegiate conferences across the Northeast, from New York City to universities like Brown and American, and even conferences at the United Nations.

Throughout the year, we host practices, workshops, and two annual conferences: miniMUNC in the fall and StuyMUNC in the spring.

Questions? Email stuyvesanthsmodelun@gmail.com

What StuyMUN Offers

Inspired by the best parts of conferences like SciMUNC, our team focuses on strong training, inclusive culture, and dynamic committees.

TRAINING-FOCUSED

Building confident speakers

We run regular practices and workshops on procedure, resolution writing, and public speaking to support both new and experienced delegates.

ACCESSIBLE

Welcoming all experience levels

Whether miniMUNC is your first conference or you are a circuit veteran, StuyMUN offers committees and coaching tailored to your level.

REALISTIC

Serious about diplomacy

Our committees emphasize real-world policy, historical context, and UN-style procedure while still keeping debate engaging.

CREATIVE

Forward-thinking committees

From alternate history to crisis simulations, delegates explore how decisions shape our world and future.
