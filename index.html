WHO WE ARE

StuyMUN is Stuyvesant High School's Model United Nations team. We are student-run and provide a community for students interested in public speaking, diplomacy, and international affairs.

Each year we attend local and collegiate conferences across the Northeast and host two conferences of our own: miniMUNC in the fall and StuyMUNC in the spring.
