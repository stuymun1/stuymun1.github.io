Fall Novice Conference

miniMUNC

miniMUNC is Stuyvesant's fall conference that typically occurs toward the end of October. It is a novice conference geared towards new delegates in order to situate them in the world of Model UN, with workshops and committee sessions throughout the day.

miniMUNC 2025 · November 8 Location · Stuyvesant High School

Conference Overview

Before the first committee session, delegates attend workshops on networking, resolution writing, and public speaking, followed by debate in a range of GA, Specialized, and Crisis committees.
